Wireless IMU Communication Chip

Fall 2023

For the team project in Fall, 2023 semester, our team designed wireless inertial measurement unit (IMU) chip utilizing SPI and UART communication protocol.
The chip is designed using Verilog, simulated with modelsim. After simulation in modelsim, it is then physically designed and DRC, LVS checked through Virtuoso and Calibre.
